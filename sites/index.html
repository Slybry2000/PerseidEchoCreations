Questions

 

I don't have a logo or photos — can you still do it?

Absolutely. We build from your public business info to start, and we can add your logo and photos anytime — just send them over and we'll drop them in.

I already have a Facebook page. Do I need this?

A site you own shows up on Google, works when Facebook is down, and looks more established to a new customer. It complements your Facebook page rather than replacing it.

Do I own it? What if I want to cancel?

Your site is hosted and maintained by us as part of the monthly plan, and you can cancel anytime. If you ever leave, we can export your site for you so nothing's held hostage.

How fast is it?

Live within about a week of claiming — usually sooner. You'll get a preview to approve before anything goes public.

Can I change it later?

Yes. Text and photo updates are included in your monthly plan. Bigger changes we'll quote up front — no surprises.
