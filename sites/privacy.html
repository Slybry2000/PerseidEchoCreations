Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Plain English: we only collect what we need to build your website and answer you, we don't sell your data, and you can ask us to delete it any time. Here are the details.

Who we are

This site is run by Perseid Echo Creations, a small business that builds and hosts websites for local businesses. For any privacy question or request, email Bryan@perseidechocreations.com.

What we collect

  • What you send us in a form — your business name, email address, and (if you provide it) phone number, when you ask us to build your site.
  • Your IP address and browser info — automatically recorded when you submit a form, so we can prevent spam and abuse.
  • Basic website analytics — aggregate page-view and traffic data so we know how the site is being used. This may use cookies.

Why we collect it

  • To reply to you and build and run the website you asked for.
  • To keep our forms free of spam and bots.
  • To understand which pages people read so we can improve the site.

What we don't do

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't share it with anyone for their own marketing. We use a small number of trusted service providers (for hosting, form delivery, and analytics) only to run this service.

How long we keep it

We keep what you submit for as long as you're a customer or an active lead. The technical details we log to fight abuse (like your IP address) are kept for a short window and then automatically deleted. If you ask us to delete your information, we will.

Your choices

You can ask us for a copy of what we have on you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email Bryan@perseidechocreations.com with your request and we'll respond within a few business days.

If you're a California resident, you have the right to know what we collect, to request deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising those rights. We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

Children

This site is meant for businesses, not children. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you think a child sent us something, email us and we'll delete it.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions or requests: Bryan@perseidechocreations.com
