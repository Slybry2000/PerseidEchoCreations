§ I

What we hold ourselves to.

Three commitments. Steady on purpose. The kind that compounds.

Goal → Strategy → Tactic.

Every campaign decomposes the same way. Nothing happens without a goal that ties to a business outcome you actually have.

Operator-in-the-loop.

The system proposes. You commit, revise, or send it back to its jurisdiction. It does not act without you. That is the whole shape of it.

Evidence over claims.

Every output is traceable to its inputs. No black-box “AI did it.” You can read the whole pipeline, top to bottom, in one sitting.

§ II

What this is not.

Most of what gets sold as marketing software is sold to a different reader. We’d rather say so plainly than imply otherwise.

Anti-positioning
  • Not a growth-hacking tool.
  • Not unreviewed AI slop.
  • Not a one-and-done campaign that goes quiet.
  • Not hype — just steady, relentless presence, run for you.

It’s the unglamorous work of showing up, every week, done well — and made to keep working.

§ III

What it actually does.

A season, in plain language. The same shape every time.

You give it your roots — your offer, your proof, your brand voice, your constraints. Bristlecone reads them once and remembers.

It plans a season — a focused campaign cycle. It drafts the cones — the campaigns — and shows them to you before anything ships.

You commit, revise, or push back. When you commit, the system posts through real channels. Real posts, on your real platforms, on schedule. No “scheduled but never shipped” theater.

Every decision and every outcome is recorded in your rings. Season over season, the picture sharpens.
