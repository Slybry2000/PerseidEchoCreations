Who it’s for.

Bristlecone is built for operators who want their marketing handled — on-brand, on schedule, without losing their say.

You’re who we built this for

Operators who want it handled.

  • A solo operator or small team running a business you intend to keep for decades.
  • A trades business, professional service, craft maker, or long-tail SaaS.
  • Ready to show up consistently — and happy to have it run for you.
  • Comfortable approving a draft before it goes out.
  • Want your hand on the wheel, without doing the driving.
  • More interested in steady results than vanity dashboards.
What working with us looks like

Marketing that runs for you.

  • Content created in your voice, every week.
  • Posted for you, on the platforms your people use.

  • Live in days — and it never goes quiet.
  • One operator who knows your business, not a ticket queue.

If that sounds like you, you’re exactly who we want to work with.
