The Green Felt Club

A smarter way to track, compare, and improve your pool game, without league bureaucracy.

Visit Website
01

Track matches and skill progression over time.

Every frame you log builds your long-term trend line. See how you improve week over week, not just who won last Tuesday.

02

Compare performance without league friction.

No captains, no weekly schedules, no admin. Just pick up a cue, log the game, and your stats catch up.

03

Enable friendly competition with meaningful stats.

Head-to-head records, venue-specific form, and handicapped leaderboards make small rivalries feel as real as any league.

04

Built where it counts — auth, data, and offline.

Flutter client with Firebase Auth and Firestore. Offline-first session logging, proper schema migrations, and no abandoned test accounts in production.

About Me

Indie "vibe coder" — defined honestly.

Perseid Echo Creations is my personal studio name for the apps I ship. It's just me.

I spent 21 years inside a large company. Now I'm building.

My focus is on AI agentic systems — designing workflows where AI doesn't just assist, but actually carries work forward. Systems that:

  • generate ideas
  • critique and improve them
  • route decisions
  • and produce real, usable outputs

Alongside that, I'm developing digital products (AaaS / SaaS), including The Green Felt Club.

The biggest shift hasn't been learning AI. It's been changing how I think about work:

Less doing everything myself.
More designing systems that do the work with me.

Still early. Building in public. Getting better every week.
