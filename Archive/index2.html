The studio

Perseid Echo Creations is run by Bryan Piard, full-time. Background in product, infrastructure, and AI engineering — most recently at T-Mobile, before going independent to build software at his own cadence.

The studio's bet is simple: small, well-made products compound over years if you build them with care and stop trying to win the quarter. Every product here is one Bryan personally wanted to exist, built with AI and a stubborn attention to the details most people skip.
